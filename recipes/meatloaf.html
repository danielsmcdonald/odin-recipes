Meatloaf

Just like your mom makes for me every night.

Meatloaf is as American as apple pie.

You ever had sex with a Meat Loaf??? Not bad.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Milk
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ketchup
  • Brown sugar
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Mix loaf ingredients.
  2. Transfer to pan.
  3. Mix sauce ingredients.
  4. Pour sauce over loaf.
  5. Bake until no longer pink in the center.
