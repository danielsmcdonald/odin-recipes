Tacos

Muy caliente! Gracias ;p

These tacos will take south to the border!!!

A lightweight but hearty snack to increase your constitution!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup diced carrots
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced cabbage
  • Corn tortillas
  • Monterey Jack cheese
  • Shredded beef birria
  • Chopped white onion
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Lime cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Make the consomme.
  2. Make the tacos.
  3. Fold the tacos.
  4. Serve the tacos with garnish.
