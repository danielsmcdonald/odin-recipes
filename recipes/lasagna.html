Lasagna

Looks good doesn't it?? You're a little piggy, arent you?

Lasagna... what more can we say about it? It speaks for itself, if you think about it... It was invented by an Italian.

Please consider making this classic Italian dish if you have the time or interest!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodle
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make sauce.
  2. Cook noodle.
  3. Make ricotta mixure.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
